Privacy Policy

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information you provide when using this website.

Last Updated: April 1, 2026

1. Information Collection

Text Tools Suite (hereinafter referred to as "this website," located at realpine.top) is an online tools website. During your use of this website, we may collect information through the following methods:

1.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit this website, we may automatically collect the following information through third-party analytics services:

  • Your IP address (anonymized)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system information
  • Access time and date
  • Pages viewed and features used
  • Referrer URL (how you arrived at this website)
  • Device type (desktop/mobile)

1.2 Information You Provide Voluntarily

If you send us a message through the contact page, we may collect the email address and message content you provide. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry.

1.3 Tool Data Processing

Important Notice: All tools provided by this website (JSON visualizer, link extractor, QR code generator, etc.) run entirely in your browser locally. The JSON data, text content, and other data you input are never transmitted to our servers, nor are they stored or recorded. All data processing is completed entirely on the client side.

2. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

This website uses the following third-party services that may set cookies on your device:

2.1 Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to analyze website traffic and user behavior. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymous statistical information that helps us understand how users interact with the website so we can improve our services.

  • Google Analytics cookies do not collect personally identifiable information
  • You can opt out of tracking by installing the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on
  • For details on how Google uses data, please see the Google Privacy Policy

2.2 Google AdSense

This website uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense may use cookies and web beacons to show you relevant ads based on your previous visits to this website or other websites.

  • Google uses the DART cookie to serve ads to users based on their visited websites
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings
  • For more information about Google's advertising technologies, visit Google Advertising Technology Policies

2.3 Local Storage

This website uses browser local storage (Local Storage) to save your theme preferences (such as dark mode selection). This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to the server.

3. Use of Information

The information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Improving website services: Analyzing usage data to optimize features and user experience
  • Displaying advertisements: Showing relevant ads through Google AdSense to sustain website operations
  • Responding to inquiries: Using your contact information to reply to your questions and suggestions
  • Security maintenance: Monitoring and preventing malicious activity to ensure normal website operation

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties, except in the following circumstances:

  • With third-party service providers you have explicitly consented to (such as Google Analytics, Google AdSense)
  • When required by law or legal process
  • When necessary to protect our rights, property, or safety

5. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the security of your information, including:

  • Using HTTPS encrypted transmission to protect data communications
  • Providing DDoS protection and network security services through Cloudflare
  • Regularly reviewing and updating security measures

Please note, however, that no method of data transmission or storage on the internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • Right to be informed: Know how we collect and use your personal information
  • Right of access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Right to rectification: Request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to opt out: Choose to opt out of cookie tracking and personalized advertising

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us through the Contact page.

7. Third-Party Links

This website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external websites. We recommend that you review the privacy policy of each website you visit after leaving our site.

8. Children's Privacy

This website is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and discover that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete that information.

9. Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The updated policy will be posted on this page with the latest update date noted. We recommend that you review this policy periodically to stay informed of any changes. Continued use of this website constitutes acceptance of the updated privacy policy.
